Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?
Deposits are non-refundable, but we allow rescheduling up to 48 hours before the event subject to availability.
Can the mermaids actually swim?
Yes! All our mermaids are trained swimmers and can perform in pools. We do require a brief safety check of your pool beforehand.
Do you host parties at your own venue?
Piratica is a mobile entertainment service. We bring the magic directly to your home, rented pool, or chosen venue!
